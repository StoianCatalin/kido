About Kimo
Kimo is one of the best platform for watching your kids in the world.

Thank you for using Kimo!

Kimo is a web system designed to help our users monitor their children in real time. Our special design provides easy user-experience so that it can be accesible to everybody, regardless of age and gender.
We provide real time information about the location on the map of the child or group of children, alongside with a series of danger notifications in the event of them leaving a safe space set by the user. Also, notifications will be sent regarding possible collisions with objects (predefined, that present a certain amount of danger).
Kimo also provides an interface of running the monitored kids, giving the possibility of finding out with whom they interact.
